# Echoes of the Abyss

Welcome to **Echoes of the Abyss**!

You play as a lone miner working in a mysterious underground cavern. Your objective is simple: gather enough wealth to escape and retire. 

### 🎯 Goal
Collect **1000 Gold** from the mine and speak to the **Blacksmith** in town to win the game!

### ⌨️ Controls
- **Move:** `Arrow Keys` (Up, Down, Left, Right)
- **Mine Gold:** Hold `C` near a gold coin to swing your pickaxe
- **Interact:** `Space`(to enter the mine or talk to the blacksmith)

### ⛏️ How to Play
1. Start in the **Town** and press `Space` near the "Mine Entrance" sign to go underground.
2. In the Mine, walk up to gold coins and hold `C` for 2 seconds to break them and collect gold.
3. Once you reach 1000 gold, click "Return to Town" at the top left.
4. Walk to the **Blacksmith** and press `Space` to complete your quest!

Have fun mining!
